HISTORY

OUR History

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate Christian students, MOI UNIVERSITY CHRISTIAN UNION began as a small Bible study group meeting in dormitories.
Today we have grown into a thriving community of over 2000 active members across multiple universities, organizing regular worship experiences, Bible studies, community outreach programs and academic support initiatives.
Our journey has been marked by countless testimonies of transformed lives, strengthened faith and academic achievements among our members.
